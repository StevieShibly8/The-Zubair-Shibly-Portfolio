Introduction

Welcome to My Story

Hi, I’m Zubair Shibly, and this is the place where I share my journey—both the milestones and the quiet moments that have shaped who I am today. From my early years growing up in Dhaka, Bangladesh, to my adventures studying abroad in Canada, I’ve always been driven by curiosity, passion, and a desire to grow. Here, you’ll find stories from my life, lessons I’ve learned, and insights into what drives me forward.

Outside of my academic and professional pursuits, I’m passionate about a variety of things. I’m an avid football fan and enjoy following both the sport and its community. In my downtime, you can catch me watching anime or getting lost in movies and TV shows that inspire new ways of thinking. I also have a deep interest in physics and love exploring how the universe works on both a macro and micro level.

I’m constantly fascinated by new technologies and artificial intelligence, and I’m always eager to stay up to date with the latest developments in these fields. Whether I’m coding, studying, or diving into the latest tech innovations, I believe in staying curious and always pushing myself to learn something new. This site is a glimpse into my experiences, challenges, and achievements. Thanks for stopping by and being part of this journey with me.

Education

Middle School & High School

I spent the majority of my youth at DPS STS School, now known as Glenrich International School, a place that shaped much of who I am today. From grade 5 to 12, this school was my second home. It was here that my passions truly took root. My love for Physics and STEM—two fields that continue to inspire me—blossomed within its walls. Whether I was engrossed in a lesson or tinkering with something new, that environment nurtured my curiosity and pushed me to explore.

But it wasn't all academics. When I wasn’t in class, you could find me at the outdoor court, playing soccer with friends who’ve become lifelong companions. The friendships I built there, forged on the field and in the classroom, remain strong, and they played a vital role in making me the person I am today.

My time at DPS wasn’t just about learning from books—it was about shaping my future. Those years taught me about determination, teamwork, and the pursuit of my passions, lessons I carry with me every day. Looking back, it's impossible not to feel a deep sense of nostalgia for those moments, those friendships, and the experiences that set me on the path I’m on now.

University

My university years at Toronto Metropolitan University (formerly Ryerson University) were transformative. It was here that I delved deeper into my chosen field of study, surrounded by like-minded individuals and inspiring professors. The rigorous academic environment pushed me to excel and think critically.

One of the highlights of my university experience was participating in co-op programs, where I gained hands-on experience working in Software Development with companies like Soti and Dayforce. These opportunities allowed me to apply theoretical knowledge to real-world projects, enhancing my skills and deepening my understanding of the industry. The experience not only bolstered my technical abilities but also fueled my passion for creating innovative solutions to complex problems.

As I enter my final year at TMU, I can’t help but reflect on the incredible journey it has been. The challenges, the friendships, and the countless lessons have all contributed to my growth, both personally and professionally. While this chapter of my life is nearing its end, I know the experiences and connections I’ve made here will continue to shape my future. The excitement of what lies ahead is fueled by everything I’ve learned and the memories I’ve created along the way.
